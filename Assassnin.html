Assassin
Characters

The Assassin will carry 2 daggers, that specialise in applying a debuff to enemies that will allow the Assassin to deal extremely high crit damage. However, the daggers cannot crit so the Assassin relies on his high damaging abilities to make use of his crit chance.

Abilities

  • Passive ability “Death’s veil”- When invisible attacking breaks the invisibility, all attacks that break invisibility deal double crit damage, all abilities used while invisible also apply a crit chance debuff on enemies.

  • Devastating strike - Teleport to an enemy if they are below a certain amount of health to instantly kill them, if the killing blow crits go invisible for a short duration.

  • It's Raining Daggers - Summon daggers that rain from the sky dealing damage in an area, if the daggers kill an enemy with a crit it causes the enemy to explode and spreads a debuff weaking enemy defences

  • Silent Execution - This is a one strike ability with a huge crit damage multiplier and can only be used when behind an enemy, if this ability one shots an enemy it spreads fear to all nearby enemies

  • Dagger Dash (Double D's) - Dash through enemies leaving a shadow clone behind that explodes after a short duration, if the dash kills an enemy with a crit it resets the cooldown of the ability

  • Flourishing crits - once active all crits regenerate hp based on the damage dealt and deal massively increased damage until the ability ends, if any crits you deal one shot an enemy reset the ability timer
