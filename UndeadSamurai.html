Undead Samurai
Characters

The Undead Samurai wields a cursed katana becoming more powerful the lower the Samurais health is. The samurai balances abilities that reduce his hp to gain a damage boost and other bonuses with abilities that will heal him to keep himself in battle.

Abilities

  • Passive “Death Bound” – The lower your HP the more damage you deal capping at 1% HP.
    Additionally upon death revive with 50% HP once every 3 minutes.

  • Blood Moon Parry - Perfectly timed parries negate an attack and counter with a bleed-inflicting slash.
    If used below 50% HP, heals for a portion of the damage negated.
    If used below 25% HP, the counterattack becomes a guaranteed crit, decapitating weaker enemies.

  • Seppuku - Slash yourself, sacrificing 50% of your current HP to gain a massive temporary damage boost. The lower your HP after using this, the higher your attack speed and movement speed is for a short duration.
    If below 25% HP, attacks during this buff have a chance to make attacks deal twice as much damage.

  • Phantom step - Dash forward phasing through enemies and dealing damage over time to those passed through.
    If below 50% HP, gain increased movement speed for a short duration.
    If below 25% HP, the ability becomes instant and resets cooldown if it kills an enemy.

  • Wailing spirits - The Samurai unleashes a cursed war cry, dealing damage and terrifying enemies in an area.
    If below 50% HP, the ability inflicts weakness, making enemies take more damage.
    If below 25% HP, the fear effect lasts longer, and all attacks crit for a short duration.

  • Blood Oath – The Samurai channels soul energy into a devastating arcing slash in front of him. The damage doubles if below 25% HP.
    If this ability kills an enemy, instantly restore a large portion of health and reduce cooldowns.
