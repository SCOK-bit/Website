Clockwork Engineer
Characters

The Clockwork Engineer wields an arcane-infused pistol and a mechanical gauntlet, relying on gadgets, turrets, and time manipulation to be effective in combat. While his basic attacks are weak, his abilities become more powerful over time through Overcharging, allowing for devastating burst damage and utility-based combos.

Abilities

  • Passive “Tactical Genius”- Every ten seconds, your next ability has no cost or cooldown and becomes Overcharged, gaining enhanced effects.

  • Overclocked Strike – Dash forward and strike an enemy with an electrified wrench, dealing massive damage.
    If Overcharged, electrify enemies in a small range on impact, also shortens ability cooldowns for all abilities by 50% of their maximum cooldown.

  • Clockwork Turret - Summon a temporary Clockwork Turret that automatically attacks enemies.
    If Overcharged, fires homing explosive rounds.

  • Temporal Rewind – Return to your position 5 seconds ago, resetting your HP and ability cooldowns to what it was 5 seconds ago.
    If Overcharged, also grants brief invincibility.

  • Gears of Annihilation – Launch a spinning gear that ricochets between enemies.
    If Overcharged, causes each hit to explode.

  • Time Fracture - Channel energy into a time rift, slowing all enemies in an area.
    If Overcharged, all the damage done to enemies in the rift is applied again right before the rift ends.
